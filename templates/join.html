How Do I Join CSA?

All students are welcome to sign up to our mailing list. However, we do offer memberships for $15 which includes a discount card to our events, as well as discounts to local sponsors and those in other cities (ie. Toronto, Markham, Scarborough, Hamilton, etc). Please see our Discounts page for more detail.



If you would like to join the executive team and help out CSA, please go to the Exec Team tab for more information.
